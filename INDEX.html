Computer components are the hardware parts that make up a personal computer (PC). They play an important role on making a good spec PC that can perform various tasks efficiently and reliably. Some of the main components of a PC are :
The case:
This is the enclosure that houses most of the other components. It comes in different sizes and shapes, depending on the type of PC you want to build. The case also contains fans and vents for cooling and airflow.

The power supply unit (PSU):
This is what converts AC to DC power from your home socket to components inside the PC. It also provides protection from surges and spikes. The PSU should have enough capacity to handle all the power demands of your PC, plus some extra for overclocking and future upgrades.

The motherboard:
-This is the main circuit board that connects all the other components together. It also contains slots for expansion cards, sockets for CPU and RAM, ports for peripherals, and BIOS for booting up. The motherboard determines what kind of CPU, RAM, GPU, and storage you can use.

The central processing unit (CPU):
This is the brain of your PC that executes instructions and calculations. It has multiple cores that can run multiple tasks simultaneously. The CPU affects how fast your PC can run programs, games, and multitasking. You should choose a CPU that suits your needs and budget.

The random access memory (RAM):
This is where your PC stores data temporarily while it is being processed by the CPU. It allows your PC to access data faster than from storage devices. The more RAM you have, the more programs you can run at once without slowing down your PC.

The graphics processing unit (GPU):
This is a specialized chip that handles graphics rendering and display output. It is essential for gaming, video editing, 3D modeling, and other graphics-intensive tasks. You can either use an integrated GPU that comes with your CPU or motherboard, or a discrete GPU that comes as a separate card.

The storage:
This is where your PC stores data permanently such as files, programs, games, etc. You can use either a hard disk drive (HDD) or a solid state drive (SSD) or both. HDDs have more capacity but are slower and noisier than SSDs which have less capacity but are faster and quieter.
These are some of the basic components of a PC that affect its performance and functionality. By choosing them wisely according to your needs and budget, you can build your own good spec PC.üòäüòäüòäüòäüòä

Full Stack Developer:
A full stack developer is someone who can work on both the front-end and back-end of a web application. The front-end is the part of the web application that interacts with the user, such as the layout, design, and functionality. The back-end is the part of the web application that handles the data, logic, and security. A full stack developer needs to have skills in various technologies and languages, such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP, Ruby, Python, Node.js, MongoDB, SQL, etc. A full stack developer can create a complete web application from scratch or maintain and improve an existing one. To become a full stack developer, one needs to learn the fundamentals of web development and then master different frameworks and tools that are used for front-end and back-end development. Some examples of popular frameworks and tools are React.js, Angular.js, Vue.js for front-end development and Express.js, Laravel, Django for back-end development. A full stack developer also needs to have good communication skills and problem-solving abilities to work effectively with clients and other developers
